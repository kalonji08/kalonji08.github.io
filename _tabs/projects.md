## African Population Genomics Framework

**Status:** In progress  
**Stack:** Python · R · PLINK · GATK · Nextflow

Developing computational pipelines for population-scale genomic analysis of African cohorts, with the aim of improving variant interpretation in understudied populations and informing precision medicine.

---

## Multi-omics Integration for Microbial Communities

**Status:** Completed (PhD)  
**Stack:** R · Python · QIIME2 · MaxQuant · Bash

Integrated 16S rRNA amplicon sequencing, metagenomics, and LC-MS/MS proteomics to characterise microbial communities and their functional profiles. Work completed as part of my PhD at the University of the Witwatersrand.

---

## Genome Assembly & Scaffolding Workflows

**Status:** Completed  
**Stack:** SSPACE · Pilon · Bash · Linux

Built and documented reproducible workflows for *de novo* genome assembly, scaffolding, and polishing — see the [blog posts](/categories/bioinformatics/) for step-by-step guides.
